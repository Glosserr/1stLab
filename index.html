Earth

Earth, the third planet from the Sun, is a vibrant world teeming with life, thanks to its breathable atmosphere, abundant liquid water, and dynamic ecosystems. Earth's rotation and axial tilt create distinct seasons, while its gravitational relationship with the Moon drives ocean tides and stabilizes the planet's climate. As humanity's home, Earth is unique in its ability to sustain life, making it a beacon of biodiversity in the vastness of space.
